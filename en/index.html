Did You Know?

Film VS Book In the book, Katniss and Gale are described as having olive skin and black hair. However, in the movie, they both have fair skin and brown hair.

In the book, Haymitch is described as being portly with curly black hair and pale gray eyes, but in the movie, he is slim, with straight blond hair and light blue eyes.

The book says Katniss is shorter than most of the tributes, but in the movie, she is taller than most of them, even some of the Careers.
